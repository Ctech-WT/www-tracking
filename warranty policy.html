Please Note: Neon Colours
Neon colors let our bikes really stand out. Nevertheless, please be aware that neon colors may lose part of their intensity when exposed to direct sunlight over a longer period.

Therefore, we suggest not to expose the bikes unnecessarily to direct sunlight.

Should the bikes be on display, it is recommended either to use UV sun protection foil for the display windows or not to put them directly at the sunny place.

We ask for your understanding that warranty does not apply to color fading.
